Chartered in 1977 as a non-profit tourism, promotion, and development agency, the Northeast Tennessee Tourism Association serves and supports Carter, Greene, Hancock, Hawkins, Johnson, Sullivan, Unicoi, and Washington counties, as well as portions of Southwest Virginia. The organization serves as one of nine statewide regional tourism organizations recognized by the Tennessee Department of Tourist Development.

Ranked by a panel of judges within the tourism and advertising industry, Pinnacle Awards were given to the top entries in each category with Merit Awards for additional entries that received high remarks.
